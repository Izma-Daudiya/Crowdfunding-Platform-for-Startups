Privacy Policy of Qrowd

At Qrowd, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our equity-based crowdfunding platform. By accessing or using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1.)Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, Aadhar Card.

  • Financial Information from Startups: Financial Statements and Business Plan.

  • Pitch Deck from Startups for Investors.

2.)How we use your information ?

  • To verify your identity and comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

  • To facilitate investments and manage your account.

  • To communicate with you about your investments and platform updates.

  • To improve our platform, services, and user experience.

  • To analyze user behavior and trends for business insights.

3.)Sharing Your Information

  • Regulatory Authorities: As required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

  • Affiliates: With companies within our corporate group for internal business purposes.

4.)Your Rights

  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information, subject to legal obligations.

5.)Children's Privacy

  • Our platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

6.)Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our platform, and the revised policy will take effect immediately upon posting.

7.)Contact Us

  • If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
